  2. Which natural bluffs arrive here?
  3. Does this player type use them?

MENTAL PERFORMANCE

Protect the quality of your decisions.

Your stop-loss is not only money. Three major errors or clear tilt means the session is over.
01

Before session

  • Name one technical focus.
  • Rate energy and emotional load.
  • Accept variance before opening tables.
02

During session

  • After a failed bluff, do not chase.
  • Pause on range-shifting turns.
  • Mark hands; do not solve them live.
03

End session

  • Stop after three major errors or tilt.
  • Label A/B/C-game honestly.
  • Write one correction for next time.
